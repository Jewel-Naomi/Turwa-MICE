Uganda

A country in East Africa with landscapes ranging from snow caped mountains to the Great Rift Valley.

Famously called the Pearl of Africa by Winston Churchill

It is home to one of the most diverse and concentrated ranges of African fauna including the highly endangered mountain gorilla and the endangered common chimpanzee.

Attractions

Most travellers come to Uganda for the gorilla safari, but other major attractions are the chimpanzees, ornithology, trekking the Rwenzoris and visiting the source of The Nile river.
